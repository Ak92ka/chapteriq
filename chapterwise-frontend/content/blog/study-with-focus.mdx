Staying focused while studying can be challenging, but these techniques can help:

<Highlight>Set clear goals for each study session.</Highlight>

Use **Pomodoro technique**: 25 minutes study + 5 minutes break.

<Highlight>Remove distractions: phone, notifications, noisy environments.</Highlight>

Review your notes using **active recall and spaced repetition**.

Happy studying!
